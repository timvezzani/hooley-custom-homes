Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2007

Hooley Custom Homes was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create exceptional custom homes that perfectly blend with Central Utah's distinctive landscape and reflect the unique personalities of the families who live in them.

With deep roots in Sanpete County, our family has been building and crafting for generations. What began as a small remodeling business has grown into one of the region's most respected custom home building companies, known for uncompromising quality and personalized service.

Our founder's background in traditional craftsmanship, combined with forward-thinking building techniques, has established a company culture where innovation meets time-honored building practices. This approach has allowed us to create homes that honor Central Utah's rich architectural heritage while incorporating modern amenities and energy-efficient features.

Our Approach

Building Relationships First

At Hooley Custom Homes, we believe that successful projects begin with strong relationships. Before we draw a single line or hammer a single nail, we invest time in getting to know you—your lifestyle, preferences, needs, and dreams for your home. This relationship-centered approach allows us to create spaces that reflect who you are and how you live.

Transparent Process

Throughout your project, you'll enjoy complete visibility and regular communication. Our systematic approach eliminates surprises and ensures you're informed and involved at every stage:

  1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your vision, needs, timeline, and budget
  2. Design Development: Translating your ideas into detailed plans and specifications
  3. Pre-Construction Planning: Finalizing selections, obtaining permits, and preparing for construction
  4. Construction: Building your project with skilled craftsmanship and regular updates
  5. Completion: Final inspections, walk-through, and ongoing support

Local Expertise

Our deep understanding of Central Utah's unique building environment—from local building codes to weather considerations—allows us to navigate challenges that builders from outside the area might miss. We've cultivated relationships with the region's best subcontractors, suppliers, and artisans to ensure every aspect of your project meets our exacting standards.

Quality Without Compromise

We never cut corners. From the foundation to the finishing touches, we use only premium materials and proven construction techniques. This commitment to quality ensures your home will stand the test of time and provide enduring value for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets Hooley Custom Homes apart from other builders in Central Utah?

What distinguishes us is our deep local roots in Sanpete County combined with our commitment to both traditional craftsmanship and modern building science. We bring generations of experience building in Central Utah's unique climate and terrain. Our relationship-focused approach means we limit the number of projects we take on to ensure each client receives the personalized attention they deserve. Additionally, our transparent process, quality-first mindset, and dedication to integrity have earned us a reputation as one of the region's most trusted builders.

How long does it take to build a custom home?

The timeline for building a custom home in Sanpete and surrounding counties typically ranges from 6-12 months, depending on the size, complexity, and time of year construction begins. Weather conditions in Central Utah can impact construction schedules, particularly during winter months. At Hooley Custom Homes, we provide a detailed construction timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated on progress throughout the building process.

What is the price per square foot for a custom home?

The cost per square foot varies widely based on many factors including site conditions, design complexity, finishes, and current material costs. In Central Utah, custom homes typically range from $250-$450+ per square foot. Rather than quoting a generic price per square foot, we prefer to discuss your specific project requirements to provide a more accurate estimate. This approach ensures transparency and helps avoid unexpected costs as your project progresses.

Do I need an architect to design my custom home?

While having an architect is beneficial, it's not always necessary. At Hooley Custom Homes, we offer design-build services where we can handle both the design and construction of your home. For more complex or unique designs, we can recommend qualified architects in the Central Utah area who understand local building traditions and code requirements. We're experienced in working with architects and can collaborate effectively throughout the design and construction process.

How much input will I have during the construction process?

At Hooley Custom Homes, we believe your involvement is crucial to creating a home that truly reflects your vision. We maintain open communication throughout the project, with regular meetings and updates. Our online project management system allows you to track progress, review schedules, and approve selections. We encourage your input on all decisions while providing expert guidance to help you make choices that align with both your vision and budget.

What warranties do you offer?

Hooley Custom Homes provides a comprehensive 1-year workmanship warranty that covers all aspects of construction. We also pass along manufacturer warranties for materials, fixtures, and systems, many of which extend well beyond our workmanship warranty. We conduct a 6-month and 11-month warranty inspection to address any concerns proactively. Our commitment to quality doesn't end at completion—we stand behind our work and respond promptly to any warranty needs.

Do you build on lots that I already own?

Yes, we regularly build on client-owned lots throughout Sanpete, Juab, and surrounding counties. We can evaluate your property to assess site conditions, utility access, and any special considerations that might impact construction. If you haven't purchased land yet, we can assist in evaluating potential properties to ensure they're suitable for the type of home you want to build.

How are payments structured during construction?

Our payment schedule is structured around construction milestones. Typically, this includes an initial deposit followed by progress payments as specific phases are completed. This approach ensures that payments align with actual construction progress. We provide a detailed payment schedule before construction begins so you know exactly what to expect throughout the process.

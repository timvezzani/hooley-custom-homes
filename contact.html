Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Sanpete County and Juab County, including Manti, Ephraim, Mount Pleasant, Spring City, Moroni, Fountain Green, Nephi, and surrounding areas. If you're outside these areas, please contact us to discuss your project.

How long does a custom home build typically take?

The timeline for a custom home build varies depending on the size, complexity, and specific requirements of your project. Generally, you can expect a custom home to take anywhere from 6 to 12 months from design to completion. We'll provide you with a more accurate timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific project.

Do you provide financing options?

While we don't provide direct financing, we work closely with several local lenders who specialize in construction loans and can recommend options based on your needs. We're happy to coordinate with your lender throughout the building process.

Do I need to hire an architect before contacting you?

No, you don't need to hire an architect before contacting us. We offer complete design-build services and can help you from the initial concept through completion. We work with trusted architects and designers who can create custom plans based on your vision, or we can work with plans you already have.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty program. New custom homes come with a 1-year workmanship warranty and longer warranties on specific systems and components. For renovations and additions, we offer a 1-year warranty on all workmanship. We'll provide detailed warranty information as part of your project proposal.
